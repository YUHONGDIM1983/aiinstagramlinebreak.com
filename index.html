How to Get Perfect Instagram Line Breaks

Instagram's algorithm automatically removes extra line breaks and spaces to maintain a clean appearance. This is why your carefully formatted captions sometimes appear as one block of text. Our tool adds special invisible characters that Instagram respects, preserving your formatting.

To add spaces in your Instagram bio, type your bio with the desired formatting in our tool, then copy the formatted text. The invisible characters we add will force Instagram to recognize and display your line breaks correctly. This ensures your bio appears exactly as you want it to.

Instagram captions can be up to 2,200 characters, but the optimal length is between 138-150 characters. This length ensures your caption isn't cut off in users' feeds while maintaining engagement. Use our AI Assistant to analyze your caption's readability and get suggestions!
